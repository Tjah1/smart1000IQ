He went to ESU and got a PA. He started a buisness called Assad International. He helped the Maria Stark Foundation and later he was in Palestine helping kids and he got bombed. He went to the hospital and got injected with the Super Stealth Serum. He then healed and got cool powers. Then with his college physical training and smarts he made a suit and Became Stealth Man.
